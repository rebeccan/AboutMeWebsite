Uni Project - A Blog for Travellers

Being unsatisfied with other travelblogs having a lack of map location functionalities, we were a team of three students who wrote their own travelblog with Ruby on Rails.

Main Features

  • CMS system
  • Google Maps integration for location markers
  • Galleries
  • Journey categorization of posts
  • Posts can be written in a markup language
